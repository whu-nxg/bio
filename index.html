个人简介 Profile

牛晓光 教授 Prof. Xiaoguang Niu

武汉大学计算机学院，博士生导师 School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, PhD Supervisor

牛晓光教授是武汉大学计算机学院的资深教授，CCF、IEEE、ACM会员，并担任CCF物联网专委会委员及教育部创新团队成员。他的研究领域涵盖移动感知与智能计算、物联网、空间智能认知与位置服务、计算机网络、网络安全与隐私保护，以及模式识别和人工智能在移动计算与位置服务中的应用。

牛教授主持了多项重要科研项目，包括国家自然科学基金、国家重点研发计划项目子课题、湖北省自然科学基金、湖北省教改项目，以及教育部重点实验室开放基金。他作为研究骨干获得了中国测绘科技进步特等奖和北京市科技进步三等奖，并在国际期刊和会议上发表了30余篇论文，其中SCI检索论文达21篇。此外，他还担任多个国际会议和SCI期刊的审稿人。

Prof. Xiaoguang Niu is a distinguished professor at the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, and a member of CCF, IEEE, and ACM. He serves as a committee member of CCF IoT and a member of the Ministry of Education's innovation team. His research areas include mobile sensing and intelligent computing, IoT, spatial intelligence and location services, computer networks, network security and privacy protection, as well as the application of pattern recognition and AI in mobile computing and location services.

Prof. Niu has led numerous significant research projects, including the National Natural Science Foundation, sub-projects of the National Key R&D Program, Hubei Provincial Natural Science Foundation, Hubei Provincial Education Reform Project, and the Ministry of Education Key Laboratory Open Fund. As a key researcher, he has received the China Surveying and Mapping Science and Technology Progress Special Award and the Beijing Science and Technology Progress Third Prize. He has published over 30 papers in international journals and conferences, with 21 SCI-indexed papers. Additionally, he serves as a reviewer for several international conferences and SCI journals.

研究方向 Research

移动感知与智能计算 Mobile Sensing and Intelligent Computing

研究移动设备感知技术与智能计算方法，实现智能化的移动应用与服务。 Research on mobile device sensing technology and intelligent computing methods to achieve intelligent mobile applications and services.

物联网技术 IoT Technology

研究物联网系统架构、通信协议、智能感知等关键技术。 Research on IoT system architecture, communication protocols, intelligent sensing and other key technologies.

空间智能认知与位置服务 Spatial Intelligence and Location Services

研究空间信息处理、位置感知与服务等技术，实现智能化的空间认知与定位服务。 Research on spatial information processing, location awareness and service technologies to achieve intelligent spatial cognition and positioning services.

招生信息 Recruitment Information

我们课题组长期招收博士生、硕士生和本科生加入我们的研究团队。我们致力于移动感知与智能计算、物联网、空间智能认知与位置服务等领域的前沿研究，为学生提供良好的科研环境和国际交流机会。 Our research group is continuously recruiting PhD students, Master students, and undergraduate students to join our team. We are dedicated to cutting-edge research in mobile sensing and intelligent computing, IoT, spatial intelligence and location services, providing students with excellent research environment and international exchange opportunities.

招生要求 Requirements

  • 具有扎实的计算机科学基础，对科研有浓厚兴趣 Solid foundation in computer science and strong interest in research
  • 具备良好的编程能力，熟悉至少一种编程语言（如C/C++、Python、Java等） Good programming skills, proficient in at least one programming language (e.g., C/C++, Python, Java)
  • 有较强的英语读写能力，能够阅读英文文献并撰写英文论文 Strong English reading and writing skills, able to read English literature and write papers in English
  • 有相关研究经验或发表过相关论文者优先 Priority given to those with relevant research experience or published papers
