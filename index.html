个人简介 Profile

牛晓光 教授 Prof. Xiaoguang Niu

武汉大学计算机学院，博士生导师 School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, PhD Supervisor

牛晓光，武汉大学计算机学院教授，博士生导师。主要研究方向包括移动感知与智能计算、物联网技术、空间智能认知与位置服务等。在IEEE TMC、IEEE TPAMI等国际顶级期刊发表多篇论文，主持多项国家自然科学基金项目。

Xiaoguang Niu is a Professor at the School of Computer Science, Wuhan University, and a PhD supervisor. His main research interests include mobile sensing and intelligent computing, IoT technology, spatial intelligence and location services. He has published multiple papers in top international journals such as IEEE TMC and IEEE TPAMI, and has led several National Natural Science Foundation of China projects.

研究方向 Research

移动感知与智能计算 Mobile Sensing and Intelligent Computing

研究移动设备感知技术与智能计算方法，实现智能化的移动应用与服务。 Research on mobile device sensing technology and intelligent computing methods to achieve intelligent mobile applications and services.

物联网技术 IoT Technology

研究物联网系统架构、通信协议、智能感知等关键技术。 Research on IoT system architecture, communication protocols, intelligent sensing and other key technologies.

空间智能认知与位置服务 Spatial Intelligence and Location Services

研究空间信息处理、位置感知与服务等技术，实现智能化的空间认知与定位服务。 Research on spatial information processing, location awareness and service technologies to achieve intelligent spatial cognition and positioning services.
